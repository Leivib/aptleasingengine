05 // Straight answers

No fine print.

What does it cost? −

A commission for each person who moves in, agreed before we start. No per-door fee, no monthly minimum, no setup cost. If we do not fill units, you do not pay us.

Does it work with my property management software? +

It runs alongside it. Leads come to us by email, tours land on your calendar, and the signed lease and resident live in whatever system you already use. Nothing to rip out.

Is the texting legal? +

Yes. Every lead we text has reached out to you first, every message carries an opt-out, and a STOP is honored instantly and permanently. That is built in, not an afterthought.

How long does setup take? +

One kickoff call, then one to two days. We do the loading and the rules. You approve the facts, and it goes live.

Does it replace my leasing team? +

No. It answers the 8pm text your team cannot, books the tour, and hands a warm, qualified renter to a real person. Your team does the tour and closes. It stops the leads that were dying in the inbox.
