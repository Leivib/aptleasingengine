The rules the engine will not break, in writing.

Done with you, not dumped on you.

// straight answers

No fine print

What does it cost?

A commission per move-in, agreed up front. There is no per-door fee or monthly minimum. If we do not fill units, you do not pay.

What channels does it use?

Both text and email. Text carries most conversations because that is what renters answer, and email replies land inside the renter's Zillow thread. Voice is on the roadmap.

Does it work with my property management software?

Any PMS, from day one: point your listing-site lead emails at us, hand us a CSV, or push leads through our API. Native two-way syncs are rolling out starting with Rent Manager, the one the big platforms skip.

Which lead sources does it catch?

Zillow, Apartments.com, and whatever ILS you list on. New sources take about a day to add.

What about my data?

Each building's data is isolated, verified by automated tests, and backed up nightly off-site.

How long does onboarding take?

A day or two. One kickoff call with your team and we are live. Your second building goes even faster.

Does it replace my onsite team?

No. It runs the lead queue and the tour calendar so your team can run the building. Anything needing judgment goes to a person, with the whole conversation attached.
